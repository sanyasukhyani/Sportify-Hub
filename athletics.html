Overview

The athletics events at the Paris Olympics 2024 were a showcase of human endurance, speed, and skill. Athletes from around the globe competed in various track and field events, setting new records and achieving personal bests.

From nail-biting finishes in the sprint events to remarkable performances in the field events, the athletics competitions were filled with moments that captivated the audience. Each race and jump added to the legacy of the Games.

  • Women's Long Jump: A breathtaking final leap that secured the gold, marking one of the longest jumps in Olympic history.
